# SOLATX: Save Our Librarians ATX

### 📚 Defending Austin ISD School Libraries
The **SOLATX** (Save Our Librarians ATX) initiative was founded in April 2026 in direct response to proposed Austin ISD budget cuts that threaten to strip our campuses of certified librarians. We believe that school libraries are essential "safe places" and academic hubs that cannot be replaced by support roles.

---

## ✊ Our Current Mission: AISD Budget Advocacy
We are currently mobilizing to oppose the AISD proposal to reassign librarians as a cost-saving measure. This repository hosts the digital tools and outreach materials used to organize parents, students, and educators before the final budget vote in June.

### Key Goals:
* **Inform:** Providing clear data on how these cuts affect student literacy and research instruction.
* **Mobilize:** Coordinating attendance at AISD board work sessions and community feedback meetings.
* **Preserve:** Ensuring every AISD student has access to a certified Librarian, not just a shared support staff member.

---

## 📂 Repository Contents
* **/website:** Source code for [solatx.org](https://solatx.org) and [saveatxlibrarians.org](https://saveatxlibrarians.org).
* **/advocacy-kits:** Templates for emails to the Board of Trustees and talking points for public comment.
* **/data-archive:** Compiled reports on AISD's deficit and the impact of library services on Austin's youth.
